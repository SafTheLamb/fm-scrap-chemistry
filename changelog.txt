Version: 0.3.0
Date: ????
  Major Features:
    - Added compatibility recipes for Space Age planets
    - Add Methane, a new gas used for explosives and rocket fuel
    - Remove water from the recipe for Sour gas sweetening (for Vulcanus compatibility)
  Minor Features:
    - Oil cracking recipes now yield Sour gas as byproduct (can be disabled)
  Compatibility:
    - Space Age: Add Methane from carbon recipe
    - Space Age: Advanced thruster fuel now uses methane (can be disabled)
    - Space Age: Sulfuric acid geysers on Vulcanus now yield sour gas when "Modify sulfur" is enabled
    - Space Age: Add electrolyte souring to produce sour gas when "Modify sulfur" is enabled
  Graphics:
    - Updated the sour gas icon to make it more distinct from the vanilla steam icon
  Changes:
    - Explosives now use methane instead of petroleum gas and water
    - Tar liquefaction no longer produces crude oil (which broke progression on vanilla SA planets wide open)
    - Increased heavy oil from tar liquefaction from 30 to 50 (+66%)
    - Removed tar from refined concrete recipe
    - Add machine tint to rocket fuel recipe (thanks S6X for caring!)
